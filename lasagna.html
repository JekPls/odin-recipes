Lasagna

Description

Ever wanted to have that Italian family over but you are afraid of the consequences of butchering a beloved time-honered dish?

Here I am to assauge all of those fears

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Red sauce
  • Ricotta
  • Ground beef
  • Parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta water
  2. Cook the ground beef
  3. Cook the pasta
  4. Layer the pasta in the pan like so:
    1. Pasta
    2. Sauce
    3. Ricotta
  5. Bake for 30 minutes at 375F
  6. Sprinkle Parmesean
